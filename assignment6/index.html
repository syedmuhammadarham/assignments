Roses Are Red

Roses are red, violets are blue,

The sun shines brightly, in skies so true.

Mountains stand tall, touching the clouds,

Whispering winds sing, nature's sounds.

The river flows gently, its course so clear,

Reflecting the moonlight, casting no fear.

Stars twinkle above, in a velvet sky,

Guiding lost souls, as time passes by.

Love is a flame, that forever burns,

In hearts entwined, each soul yearns.

Hope springs eternal, through darkest night,

Embracing dreams, with pure delight.

Life's a canvas, awaiting our art,

Painting moments, from the depths of the heart.

So let us dance, to life's sweet tune,

Chasing rainbows, beneath the moon.
