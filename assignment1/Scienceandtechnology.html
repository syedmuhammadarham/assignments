Science and Technology

The relationship between science, art, and culture centers on enriching each other. Various technologies were developed through scientific breakthroughs to provide new ways of creating art. Consequently, new art forms like digital art, NFT art, and AI-generated art have emerged. On the other hand, art provides a creative lens for us to see and understand science from a creatic. Furthermore, science influences culture by bringing positive or negative changes to the values, belief systems and traditions of a certain population. Science is notorious for challenging norms that do not help society to move forward.

Science and technology are opening new doors to reinvent business processes, enhance and augment human decision-making. It is a core component of digital transformation and efforts towards it. Science driven roadmap relies on robust infrastructure, scaling strategies, governance, and incorporation of lawful principles.
