Chicken Manchurian Rice

Chicken Manchurian Rice is a popular Indo-Chinese dish that is made with chicken, vegetables, and rice. The chicken is typically coated in a batter made with cornstarch, soy sauce, and other seasonings, and then fried until crispy. The vegetables are typically stir-fried with the chicken, and then the rice is added and everything is cooked together until heated through. Chicken Manchurian Rice is typically served with a side of sweet and sour sauce.
