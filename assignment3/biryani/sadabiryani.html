Sada Biryani

Sada Biryani, also known as Plain Biryani, is a simple and delicious rice dish that is popular in South Asia. It is made with basmati rice, spices, and herbs, and is typically cooked in a sealed pot (dum pukht) to trap the steam and help to develop the flavors.Sada Biryani is a hearty and flavorful dish that is perfect for a special occasion or a casual weeknight meal. It can be served with a side of raita or chutney.
