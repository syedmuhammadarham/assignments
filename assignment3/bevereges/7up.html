7up

7up is a citrus-flavored soft drink that is similar to Fanta. It is available in many fruit varieties, like orange, apple, strawberry, among others. It is part of a beverage area often referred to as the flavour segment, comprising carbonated and non-carbonated fruit-flavoured beverages. The orange flavour of 7up now represents the majority of 7up sales worldwide following a major repositioning of the brand towards that flavour in the early 1990s.
