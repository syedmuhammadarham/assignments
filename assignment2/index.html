Frensco Restaurant

This restaurant is famous for its kababs and nihari, but they also have a great selection of biryanis and other dishes. The food is cooked in a traditional tandoor oven, which gives it a smoky flavor that is truly delicious it's a popular spot for both locals and tourists alike, and the prices are very reasonable..
