Chicken Burger

Chicken burger is a type of sandwich that consists of a patty made from ground chicken, served in a bun, typically hot and often with other ingredients. The chicken patty used in such a burger is typically formed into a patty and then grilled or fried. The bun is typically a hamburger bun, but other types of buns, such as Kaiser rolls or brioche buns, may also be used. Other ingredients that may be used in a chicken burger include lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard. Chicken burgers are often served with fries or onion rings as a side dish.
