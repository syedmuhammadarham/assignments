Zinger Burger

A Zinger burger is a type of chicken burger that is made with a spicy, battered chicken patty. It was first introduced by KFC in the United Kingdom in 1996, and it has since become a popular menu item around the world. The Zinger burger is made with a chicken breast that is coated in a batter made with spices, herbs, and flour. The chicken is then deep-fried until it is golden brown and crispy. The chicken patty is then placed on a bun and topped with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and a special Zinger sauce.
