Chicken Broast

Bihari Boti is a popular dish in Pakistan and India. It is made with tender chunks of beef that are marinated in a flavorful blend of spices and herbs. The beef is then grilled to perfection and served with a side of rice or naan bread. The word "bihari" refers to the state of Bihar in India, where the dish originated. The word "boti" means "chunk" in Urdu.
