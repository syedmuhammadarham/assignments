Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a rice dish made with chicken, rice, spices, and herbs. It is a popular dish in South Asia, and there are many different regional variations. The dish is typically made by layering the chicken and rice in a pot, along with spices and herbs, and then cooking it over low heat until the rice is cooked through and the flavors have melded together Chicken Biryani is a hearty and flavorful dish that is perfect for a special occasion or a casual weeknight meal. It can be served with a side of raita or chutney.
