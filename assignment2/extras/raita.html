Raita

Raita is a popular Indian side dish made with yogurt, cucumber, herbs, and spices. It is a refreshing and cooling dish that is often served with spicy or oily food. Raita can be made with a variety of vegetables, herbs, and spices, but the most common ingredients are yogurt, cucumber, mint, cilantro, and cumin.
