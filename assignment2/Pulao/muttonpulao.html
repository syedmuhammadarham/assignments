Mutton Pulao

Mutton pulao is a rice dish made with mutton, rice, spices, and herbs. It is a popular dish in South Asia, and there are many different regional variations. The dish is typically made by layering the mutton and rice in a pot, along with spices and herbs, and then cooking it over low heat until the rice is cooked through, and the flavors have melded together. Mutton pulao is a hearty and flavorful dish that is perfect for a special occasion or a casual weeknight meal. It can be served with a side of raita or chutney.
