Chicken Cheese Handi

Chicken Cheese Handi is a popular Pakistani and North Indian dish made with Chicken, onions, tomatoes, and a variety of spices. It is typically cooked in a karahi, which is a type of deep, heavy-bottomed pan that is used for stir-frying and other high-heat cooking methods. The dish is said to have originated in the Kyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan, and it is now enjoyed all over the world. It is a popular dish for both casual and formal occasions, and it can be served with rice, naan, or roti.
